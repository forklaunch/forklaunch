ForkLaunch Compliance Coverage Report

Updated March 24, 2026

ForkLaunch addresses every technical control required by HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI DSS, and GDPR at both the application and infrastructure layers. Controls are enforced across three layers: the Framework (@forklaunch/core — compile-time types, runtime middleware, encryption, tenant isolation), the CLI (forklaunch binary — code generation, compliance auditing, GitHub configs), and the Platform (ForkLaunch Cloud — risk scoring, data flow diagrams, DPIA, portal dashboard, deploy-time enforcement). Each control below is tagged with its layer. Controls that are not covered are either addressable with additional engineering work or are organizational processes that require business action.

27 Risk Analysis Addressed Platform Platform ComplianceReportService computes normalized risk score (0-100) from CLI audit data. Scoring checks: unencrypted PHI/PCI fields (10 pts), public routes (5 pts), routes without access levels (15 pts), entities with PII but no tenant isolation (10 pts). Score normalized over total items (fields + routes + entities). Risk levels: Low (≤10), Medium (≤30), High (≤60), Critical (>60). CLI displays with --risk-score flag. Portal shows in compliance dashboard. HIPAA §164.308(a)(1)
